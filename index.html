Jaiprakash Bharti (02 January, 1936 AD - 5 February, 2005) was a famous Hindi writer and former editor of Nandan . Bharti ji did important work on Hindi children's literature. He is called the creator of the era of Hindi children's literature. He gave 'Nandan' the status of the best children's magazine.

Biography

Bharti ji was born in Meerut ( Uttar Pradesh ). After passing MA, B.Sc., Sahitya Ratna examinations, he did diploma in journalism and other subjects. More than one thousand of his poems, stories and articles have been published. He edited more than a hundred books. He was felicitated by the Vice President by awarding him a silver medal for writing excellent children's literature. He was the co-editor and science editor of ' Saptahik Hindustan ' for ten years. Later he became the editor of Nandan.
